Healthcare Workforce Mental Health Analytics Dashboard
📌 Project Objective

To analyze workforce mental health patterns in healthcare organizations by visualizing stress levels, burnout frequency, job satisfaction, support access, and turnover intention using Power BI.

📊 Project Type

Descriptive and diagnostic analytics dashboard.

📂 Data Source

Healthcare Workforce Mental Health Dataset (CSV)

🛠 Tools Used

Power BI

DAX

CSV Dataset

Data Visualization

Business Intelligence

📈 Dashboard Features

KPI cards for workforce metrics

Department-wise analysis

Employee type segmentation

Workplace factor analysis

Stress vs job satisfaction visualization

Burnout distribution analysis

Turnover intention analysis

Dynamic slicers

Interactive filtering

Drill-through employee-level table

🔍 What This Dashboard Shows

Distribution of stress levels across departments

Distribution of job satisfaction across departments

Stress concentration by employee type

Burnout concentration by workplace factors

Relationship between stress and job satisfaction

Turnover intention patterns across departments

Support access distribution across roles

Workforce mental health pattern distribution

📌 Key Insights (Data-Based)

Stress levels vary significantly across departments.

Frontline roles show higher stress concentration.

Heavy workload is the dominant burnout factor.

Higher stress is associated with lower job satisfaction.

Turnover intention differs across departments.

Support access varies across employee types.

Burnout and stress are structurally clustered.
